ZKTeco to CS Form No. 48 Converter

This converter is designed and developed to efficiently transform ZKTeco biometric Daily Time Record (DTR) files into Civil Service Form No. 48. The system is carefully optimized to work exclusively with ZKTeco biometric devices, guaranteeing seamless compatibility, precise output, and dependable performance. At this time, support for other biometric brands is not available, as the system is specifically tailored to maintain the integrity and standardization of ZKTeco-generated data.
